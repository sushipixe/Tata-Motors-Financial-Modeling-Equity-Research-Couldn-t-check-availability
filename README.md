# 🚗 Tata Motors Financial Modeling & Equity Research Analysis

## 📊 Project Overview

This project presents a comprehensive Financial Modeling and Equity Research Analysis of Tata Motors Ltd., one of India's leading automobile manufacturers.

The objective of this project is to evaluate the company's financial performance, growth potential, operational efficiency, and investment attractiveness through detailed financial analysis, forecasting, and valuation techniques.

The model has been built in Microsoft Excel using historical financial data and corporate finance methodologies.

## 🏢 About Tata Motors

Tata Motors Limited is a flagship company of the Tata Group and one of India's largest automobile manufacturers.

The company operates across multiple segments, including:

- Passenger Vehicles
- Commercial Vehicles
- Electric Vehicles (EVs)
- Luxury Vehicles through Jaguar Land Rover (JLR)

Tata Motors serves customers across India and international markets while maintaining a strong presence in the evolving mobility and electric vehicle ecosystem.

- Analyze historical financial performance.
- Evaluate profitability and operational efficiency.
- Forecast future revenues and earnings.
